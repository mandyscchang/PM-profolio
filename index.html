ABOUT ME

Here's My Story

I'm an aspiring product manager who's passionate about healthcare technology with 2.5 years of experience developing and implementing unique feature opportunities for each customer based on their user needs and user challenges.

I most recently worked as a Technical Solutions Engineer at a healthcare software company (Epic Systems) where I supported 5 enterprise hospitals as customers with their software in the perioperative areas. In addition to my primary work responsibilities, I worked on software development projects to help reduce the development team’s backlog and technical debt. During my time at Epic, I also worked as a feature lead for a real-time patient status monitoring feature used in the operating rooms. I proactively supported team members on demoing, planning, and implementing the feature for their customers.

Prior to working at Epic, I worked as a Testing Engineer at a medical device research company (Cook Research Inc.) conducting research, testing, and data analysis on various medical devices and received my B.S. in Biomedical Engineering from Purdue University.

In my personal time, I love to travel and explore new restaurants. I’ve visited 3 countries and 17 states so far, and I’m looking forward to adding more places to my travel journal in the future!
